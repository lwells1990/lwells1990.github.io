Purchase Locally Grown Hops From Stone Rose Farms in Central New York

Recently in NYS the Farm Brewing Law (2012) was passed requiring local brewers in the state to purchase no less than 90% of their hops from local farmers by 2024. Stone Rose Farms Hops has been created with the intent to capitalize on this new market for local breweries in need. The main purpose of this proposed website will be to educate and provide an online marketplace for potential buyers of hops from Stone Rose Farm in Mexico, NY. Check out the Navigation Menu to the right to learn more about our farm, contact us, browse our selection of hops, and finalize your hops order.
